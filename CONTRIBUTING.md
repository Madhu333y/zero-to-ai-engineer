# Contributing to zero-to-ai-engineer

This repository is a public learning journey. 
Anyone can follow along, fork it, or contribute.

---

## How to Follow This Path Yourself

1. Fork this repository
2. Replace the progress bars with your own
3. Commit every single day
4. Post every Friday on LinkedIn
5. Tag me — I will follow your journey

---

## How to Contribute to This Repository

### Found a mistake in my code?
Open an issue and tell me exactly what broke and why.

### Have a better approach to something I built?
Open a pull request with your suggestion.
Explain why your approach is better in production.

### Want to add a resource?
Add it to the relevant file in the resources/ folder.
Must be something you have personally used — not just a link you found.

---

## The One Rule

Everything in this repository must be real.

- Real cost numbers — not estimates
- Real mistakes — not polished success stories  
- Real time spent — not what sounds impressive
- Real evaluation scores — not just the good ones

**If it did not actually happen — it does not go in.**
